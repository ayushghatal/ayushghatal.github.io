## Typography

A second-level heading marks a new section. Below it, another paragraph continues the flow. **Bold text** lifts to `#e0e0e0` so it catches the eye without screaming. *Italic text* shifts to `#aaa` and adds a quiet emphasis. You can combine them — ***bold italic*** — though it's rarely needed.

Here is a longer paragraph to test reading rhythm. Good typography isn't about individual words — it's about how the eye moves across a block of text. The line height, the letter spacing, the slight warmth of `#999` against pure black — all of it works together. This sentence is here to give the paragraph enough length to feel like real prose rather than a placeholder.
